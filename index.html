Fun fact about me...

Growing up, I hated science and mathematics! Throughout elementery and middle school, I really struggled with math and actually preferred english, reading, and writing. However, I challenged myself in high school to take AP math and physics courses and discovered a new passion of mine! Over the years, I have developed a deep appreciation for physics and a passion for applying its logic based framework to other fields. At its core, physics is really about problem solving which is a universal, transferrable skill. My time as an astrophysics major has trained by brain to think in a highly analytical manner and to develop an understanding of data-driven theories.
